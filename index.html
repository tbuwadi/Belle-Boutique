Belle Boutique is
the leading brand for
vegan, cruelty free clothing.

Established in London, Canada, Belle Boutique strives to make you feel comfortable in your skin. With a variety of styles for everyone, Belle pieces are carefully hand crafted and shipped right to your doorstep. Quality is our number one priority, so you can be confident that your pieces will last. Vegan and cruelty free, we take pride in our clothing, and we know you’ll love our brand. Shop Belle Boutique, because everyone deserves to
be-you-tiful.

Meet Olivia, Zina, and Joanne

Olivia Ron —
Product Design

Olivia Ron grew up in St. Thomas, Ontario. She's passionate about design, and wants to keep on creating more fashion ideas! You can always find her sketching new creative designs for the Belle Boutique. She is the creative face behind all the Belle Boutique Merch.

Zina Alexander —
Marketing Specialist

Zina Alexander studied marketing at the IVEY School of Business and uses her wide expertise to drive the Belle Boutique. She loves social media marketing and finding new ways to appeal to Canadians. She presents our clothing in a way to catch anyone’s attention!

Joanne Mario —
Business Executive

Joanne Mario is the founder of the boutique, and takes care of the admin and finance. She loves calculators and excel spreadsheets almost as much as she loves her 2 kids George and Ivan. Starting her first business at 15 in Toronto, she has excelled in running 2 multi-million businesses.
